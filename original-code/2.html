How Kunal works

From messy problem spaces to quietly confident products, the process stays anchored in teams, evidence and thoughtful craft.

Discovery

Make the problem smaller

Ground decisions in interviews, flows and data before sketching UI. Aligns product, engineering and ops around the same constraints and success measures.

Systems

Design once, reuse with care

Uses systems thinking and shared primitives so new features feel familiar, accessible and easy to ship across multiple products.

Collaboration

Bring teams into the room

Co‑creates with PMs, engineers and clinicians through workshops, async artifacts and design crits, keeping everyone close to patients.
