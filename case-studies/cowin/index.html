Designing for a Nation in Crisis

I led the design for a platform that navigated extreme public anxiety and technical chaos to facilitate over 2 crore vaccine certificate downloads.

1. The Setup: A High-Stakes Mandate

In 2021, India faced a monumental challenge: vaccinating its population amidst a devastating pandemic wave. The government released the Co-WIN APIs, creating a sudden, urgent opportunity. The business goal for Eka.Care was clear: leverage these APIs to build a superior, trustworthy public utility that could capture a massive, anxious user base and establish us as a key player in India's digital health ecosystem.

The User's Reality: A State of Panic

The public sentiment was not one of simple "user needs"; it was a state of crisis. My rapid research into online forums, social media, and news reports revealed the core user anxieties:

Availability Anxiety

"Will I ever find a slot for my elderly parents? I've been trying for days."

Trust & Legitimacy

"Is this third-party app even legitimate? How do I know it's safe to give them my Aadhar number?"

Information Gaps

"How do I know this information is real and not a waste of my time? Is the slot even still there?"

My Role: Designer as a De-risking Agent

As the sole designer on this initiative, I was tasked with creating a functional UX under an extreme "one-day delivery mode" timeline. With no PM, my role expanded significantly. I was responsible for de-risking the project by defining requirements, analyzing technical constraints, and creating a clear plan of action that the entire cross-functional team could rally behind.

2. Discovery: Finding Signal in the Noise

The existing official portal was functional but overwhelmed, creating a significant "trust gap." Users were desperate for an alternative that was not just faster, but clearer and more reliable. My discovery process started with the technology itself to understand our exact capabilities.

Deconstructing the Technical Foundation

My first step was a deep dive into the technical foundation—the Co-WIN APIs. Instead of just reading the docs, I deconstructed them into a clear, grouped list to understand our building blocks. This helped the entire team, including non-technical stakeholders, grasp what was possible.

Metadata APIs

Appointment Availability APIs

Certificate APIs
POST /v2/certificate/generate Generate certificate
POST /v2/certificate/addBeneficiary Add beneficiary
GET /v2/certificate/status Check certificate status

The "Search Near Me" Insight

Key Insight: While the APIs provided basic search by PIN and District, I identified a critical gap: there was no single API for a simple, location-aware "search near me." Competitors were just replicating the basic government portal forms.

My Strategic Solution: I proposed a new user flow. By first requesting the user's device location (with permission), we could programmatically determine their current Pincode and feed it into the existing API. This seemingly small step created a "one-tap" search experience that was vastly superior.

Mapping the Anxiety Journey

This technical understanding, combined with the "Search Near Me" insight, allowed me to frame the user's emotional state. I synthesized this into a journey map that became our team's guiding star.

3. The Three Pillars of Trust

Based on the journey map and user anxieties, I established three core design principles that became our unwavering guide for every feature and decision.

1

Radical Transparency

Show everything, hide nothing. To build trust, we had to be more comprehensive and forthcoming than the official sources, even if it meant showing "bad news."

2

Honest Expectations

Never overpromise. Be explicit about data freshness and potential failures to prevent the user frustration that comes from chasing non-existent "ghost" slots.

3

Anxiety-Proof Confirmation

The end of the flow must be a definitive, reassuring "contract" that contains every piece of necessary information, leaving absolutely no room for doubt.

4. Design & Execution

Guided by our three pillars of trust, I designed the two most critical user journeys: booking a slot and downloading a certificate. The execution focused on providing clarity and control at every step.

4.1 Flow: Booking a Vaccination Slot

The slot booking process was the epicenter of user anxiety. My goal was to create a flow that was not only functional but actively worked to calm the user.

1. Proactive Filtering & Absolute Transparency

Instead of hiding "bad news," we embraced it. The search results page showed all vaccination centers, clearly marking those with no available slots. This upheld our "Radical Transparency" principle and prevented users from wasting time on centers that had no availability.

2. Automated Slot Reservation

To combat "ghost slots," I designed an intermediate "booking in progress" screen. When a user selected a center, our system would attempt to hold a slot for a short period, creating a buffer that transformed the experience from frantic to controlled. This reduced booking failures by 87%.

3. The Anxiety-Proof Confirmation

The final confirmation screen was designed as an "anxiety-proof contract." It contained every critical piece of information: Hospital Name, Address, Date, Time, Beneficiary Name, leaving no room for doubt. Users could screenshot or save this confirmation for their records.

4.2 Flows: Downloading the Vaccine Certificate

The certificate download was our single biggest user acquisition channel. I designed two distinct "smart" flows to get users their certificate as frictionlessly as possible.

Explicit Path: User-Initiated

This flow serves users who know exactly what they need. With minimal form fields and clear progress indicators, it reduces cognitive load while maintaining data security through Aadhaar verification.

Background Processing

Certificate generation happens in the background, allowing users to continue using other app features while the system handles the Co-WIN API communication and validation.

Automated Path: Proactive Detection

Leveraging our user database, the system automatically detects when users become eligible for certificates based on their vaccination records. This transforms a reactive task into an delightful surprise.

Smart Notification System

When certificates are ready, users receive rich notifications with direct download links. The system handles retry logic and provides ETAs for when certificates will be available, managing expectations perfectly.

Comprehensive Key Learnings

In a Crisis, Clarity is the Most Valuable Feature

The "best" design isn't always the cleanest or most minimal; in this case, it was the most explicit and reassuring. Our radical transparency approach showed users "bad news" (no available slots) with timestamps, which actually increased trust more than hiding information. Users appreciated knowing the reality rather than chasing phantom opportunities.

Lead with Artifacts, Not Opinions

In a high-speed environment, well-designed artifacts (journey maps, flows, API documentation) became the fastest way to create alignment and de-risk development. The anxiety journey map I created became the single source of truth that guided every design decision and helped the cross-functional team understand the "why" behind our approach.

Design the Entire System, Not Just the Interface

Our biggest wins came from thinking about the entire user journey—from managing expectations *before* the search to providing value *after* the download. The automated certificate detection system turned a reactive chore into a delightful surprise, creating multiple touchpoints that kept users engaged with the platform long after their immediate need was met.

Technical Constraints Create Design Opportunities

The "Search Near Me" feature, born from understanding API limitations, became our key differentiator. By leveraging device location permissions to auto-fill PIN codes, we created a "one-tap" experience that competitors couldn't easily replicate. This taught me that constraints often breed the most innovative solutions.

Trust is Built Through Consistent Micro-interactions

Every element, from the "booking in progress" screen to the anxiety-proof confirmation, was designed to build trust. The small details—showing data freshness timestamps, providing clear error messages, and offering multiple download paths—accumulated to create a platform that users felt they could rely on during a national crisis.
